Steps to convert Your handwritten notes to document:-


  1. Register Your account (Sign Up).
  2. Take snapshots of your notes convert it into pdf.
  3. Head to your Profile and click on upload.
  4. Upload your notes in a pdf format. Also provide relevant information as deadline and notes for the typers.
  5. Enter all the details and submit.
  6. Pay the amount of coins required for conversion.(In case you don't have coins purchase some or watch ads to gain some coins)
  7. Once the coins are successfully deducted from your account, you will get the option to choose typers to apply. (In case the choosen typers don't reply for 3hrs. you will get a notification and your request will be posted on the general link.)
  8. Once the work is completed you will receive a email with a link to download your document.

How to apply for a typist?


  1. Login/Register your account.
  2. Head to your profile and go to the typist section.
  3. Click on apply for typist.
  4. Fill in the required information and agree to our terms and conditions.
  5. Once, done you will be able to view the Work page where you can pick up conversion requests.
  6. Read the information provided by the user, and if you are up for it click on accept request.
  7. Complete the work before the deadline and upload the document.
  8. After conformation from our customer we will trasfer over the points to your account.
  9. Once you accumulate a certain amount of points you can then convert them to money and transfer it to your bank account.
  10. We rank the typist according to the customer reviews and typists with poor work or who don't complete work before deadline are banned from working for 1 week. So, make sure to complete the work before deadline.
