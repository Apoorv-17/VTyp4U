This website was made in accordance to the completion of the J-Component in Internet Web Programming and under the guidance of our Teachers.

This website is designed to enable users to convert their handwritten notes to document by paying a small amount of money. This website works on the basis of the points system i.e. your money is converted into points and is then added to your account you can convert those points to money anytime you want. When you request a document conversion the point is transferred to the person typing the document for you and is thus serves as a payment for his/her hardwork.
